Privacy Policy for InstaBuddy

Last updated: November 26, 2024

Overview

InstaBuddy ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our data practices for the InstaBuddy mobile application.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

InstaBuddy operates entirely on your device. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics are tracked
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No user accounts or registration required

How Your Data is Processed

All data processing happens locally on your device:

  • Photos: Images you select remain on your device and are processed locally
  • Created Content: Carousels and exported images are saved to your device storage only
  • Settings: App preferences are stored locally using device storage
  • Custom Stickers/Watermarks: Stored locally on your device

Permissions

InstaBuddy requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: To capture photos for use in your carousels (optional)
  • Storage: To access photos from your gallery and save exported images

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and no data leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

InstaBuddy does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

InstaBuddy does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The app can be safely used by all ages as no data is collected.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. All content created in InstaBuddy remains under your complete control on your device.
